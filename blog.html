Blog

My blog is where I explore some of the nooks and crannies of the publishing world. I also delve into some of the not-so-hidden parts of the publishing sphere and take a look at the merging of print publishing with the digital world. I keep my blog up to date with my adventures applying for internships and research projects. If you're an avid reader or any sort of interested in taking a peek into the book-making world, you should check it out.

You can find me at Wordpress, or click on the "W" in the upper right-hand corner.
